At ePark, located within the Ericsson premises, we provide an efficient and easy-to-use car charging station exclusively for our employees and visitors. Our goal is to support your commitment to sustainable transportation by offering a reliable and seamless charging experience right where you work.

By using ePark, you're joining a community of eco-conscious individuals dedicated to promoting sustainable practices. Enjoy the convenience of on-site charging and contribute to a greener future.

Thank you for choosing ePark at Ericsson. Your journey to sustainable transportation starts here.

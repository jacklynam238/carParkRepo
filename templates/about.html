About Us

    Our Team

    Epark is a pioneering company that developed an innovative booking website for managing the car park outside a prominent software engineering company. This company faces a significant challenge with only 7 parking spots available for over 100 electric vehicles on campus. Epark's advanced system ensures the efficient and fair allocation of these limited parking spaces, optimising their use and minimizing congestion and frustration among employees.

    The company was founded by a dedicated team of eight talented colleagues: Peter, Dylan, Christian, Jack, Aine, Hugh, Keira, and Maedbh. Together, they combined their expertise in technology, logistics, and user experience to create a seamless and user-friendly platform. Epark's mission is to revolutionise the way parking is managed in high-demand areas, providing innovative solutions that cater to the needs of modern, eco-conscious communities.




    Our Idea

    Epark's idea is to develop an easier parking system for electric vehicle users at Ericsson, enhancing organization and supporting sustainability and eco-friendly transportation. This advanced booking system is designed to be user-friendly and creates a more convenient parking experience. It encourages the use of eco-friendly electric vehicles which promotes environmental sustainability. Epark's goal is to create an efficient and fair parking environment for electric car users and to support sustainable transportation. By addressing the needs of modern drivers and promoting eco-friendly transportation, Epark is set to enhance convenience and efficiency while contributing to Ericsson's commitment to environmental responsibility.

    Epark's innovative idea includes real-time space availability tracking, a convenient website to easily book a space, an organised parking system and much more. By implementing these features, Epark not only improves daily life for Ericsson employees but also supports sustainability within the workplace. This initiative provides a practical solution that benefits both individuals and the environment.
